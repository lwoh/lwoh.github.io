SimilarCafeBETA

Discover nearby cafe similar to what you miss in other parts of the world ...


Automated Intelligent travel itinerary planner capable of suggesting nearby attractions to help anyone explore the world.


Winner of Discover MY Apps 2014 hackathon (Category: Ministry of Tourism and Culture (MoTaC)), and shortlisted as nominee for finals of MSC Malaysia APICTA Awards 2014 (Category: Best of Tourism and Hospitality).

  • Features
  • Automatically suggest lists of nearby attractions such as restaurants, cafes, places of interest, etc.
  • Drag and drop selected attractions to construct itinerary called "timeline"
  • Save timelines in PC
  • View saved timelines in online or offline mode
  • Organize timelines by days of week and time
  • Technical Info
  • Windows 8 app for PC
  • Integrated with Foursquare Places API
