SimilarCafeBETA

Discover nearby cafes 'similar' to the place you miss ... anywhere in the world ...

Website to search for cafes within 10km of you that are 'similar' to your favourite place.


Previous Next
  • Just 2 steps & you will be shown a list of 'similar' cafes.
  • Step #1 - Allow browser to detect your current location automatically/Manually type & select from autocomplete to update your current location.
  • Step #2 - Type & select your favourite place from autocomplete at the top of the page.
  • #Done! - Browse the list of 'similar' cafes nearby you. Click any cafe on the list to view more info.
  • Features
  • Automatically detect current location/manually update current location
  • Automatically shorten search keyword & re-execute search up to 2 times
  • Google Street View + Google Map location for searched place & selected cafe (one at a time)
  • Technical Info
  • Best viewed on Google Chrome
  • Integrated with Google Maps & Google Places APIs
  • Limitations
  • Restricted to searching for cafes only (max 20)
  • Search radius = 10km
