LOOK! Planning Made Easy ...


Intelligent travel itinerary planner capable of suggesting nearby attractions to help anyone explore the world.


Winner of Discover MY Apps 2014 hackathon (Category: Ministry of Tourism and Culture (MoTaC)), and shortlisted as nominee for finals of MSC Malaysia APICTA Awards 2014 (Category: Best of Tourism and Hospitality).

  • Save timelines in PC
  • View saved timelines in online or offline mode
  • Organize timelines by days of week and time
